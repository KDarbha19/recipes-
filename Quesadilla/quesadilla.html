Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a warm, melty hug wrapped in a golden tortilla. It is a beloved Mexican dish where a tortilla—usually flour—is filled with cheese and then folded and grilled until the outside is crisp and the inside is gooey.

Ingrediants

  • tortilla
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Queso
  • butter

Steps

  1. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat and melt the butter.
  2. Lay one tortilla flat in the skillet. Sprinkle shredded cheese evenly over it, then spoon on the queso dip.
  3. Place the second tortilla on top. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes until the bottom is golden and the cheese starts to melt.
  4. Use a spatula to flip the quesadilla. Cook the other side for another 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Remove from the pan, let it cool slightly, then cut into wedges.
